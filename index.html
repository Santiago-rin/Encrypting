Encrypting





Welcome to "Encrypting", in this web application you can encrypt and decrypt texts and images with different cryptosystems such as Displacement, Substitution, Permutation, Affine, Vigenere, Hill, etc..., in addition to cryptanalysis of these systems, block ciphers and public keys. This has been done by a student of Mathematics at the National University of Colombia with the aim of verifying the learning obtained in the course "Introduction to cryptography and information theory" thanks to Professor Agustín Moreno Cañadas, who can be contacted at the e-mail address amorenoca@unal.edu.co.

Over the years, cryptography has been very useful to the world with its various functionalities, since ancient Egypt this has been used, it was very important in the first and second world war to decrypt those confidential messages where there were clear intentions to attack together, lately cryptography has made great progress in areas such as medicine, science, engineering and the creation of currencies that are increasingly being evaluated.
